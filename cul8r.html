Fun Facts

Bio: CUL8R is Water's brother, who joined the team mainly because "why not?". He is a pretty solid player, his Smash journey beginning around 3 years ago with SSBU. He plays a lot of different characters and loves to play casually with wacky rulesets he makes himself. However, just because he is a casual player doesn't mean he is bad or anything. He was trained by some of the best on the team.

Notable Characteristics: Likes to do cool stuff, so beware, or you will get styled on. Likes to have an element of luck to his playstyle, likes to call people a "chuck" when the impose a decicive victory.

Hobbies/Skills: CUL8R loves to be creative and enjoy time with insane games that he makes himself. He is also an adept artist. He is really good at making friends.

Favorite Color: Blue

Favorite Video Games (that aren't SSBU:) Boomerang Fu, Blazing Beaks, Lego Marvel Superheros, Deltarune, Minecraft, Rivals of Aether, Cuphead.
