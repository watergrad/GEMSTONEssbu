Fun Facts

Bio: Darkstorm had his first exposure to Smash Bros at a cousin’s house, with Super Smash Bros. Brawl. Later on, he started playing Super Smash Bros. for Wii U, and expanded his understanding of the base mechanics. However, things really picked up with Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, and he got fairly serious about playing. To this day, he remains one of the “Big Three” of Team Gemstone.

Notable Characteristics: Amazing counter instincts, decent with a large group of characters. Usually stays away from direct conflict, but is eager to pounce on an opportunity to start a chain of attacks.

Hobbies/Skills: Dark's hobbies include not touching grass, debate, and of course, SSBU.

Favorite Color: Red

Favorite Video Games (that aren't SSBU:) The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, Hollow Knight, Celeste, Minecraft.
