About Us

Hey there! If you are reading this, I can only assume that you are either one of us or curious about who we are. Well, to be frank, we are just some friends who enjoy playing Super Smash Bros. Ultimate. This is our website to track information about us. Everything is subject to change, and as long as VasserDrachen (top local player and the person who is writing this) updates this page, you should be seeing the most recent data from everyone on the team. We are all about getting better, and if you wanna be a part of it, we are all in!

If you would like to join our group, please contact Vasser at waterdrag567yt@gmail.com.



NEWS

The first official GEMSTONE season is underway! I am happy to announce that our team of 5, consisting of 3 main players and 2 substitutes, has been selected! We are super excited to practice and prove our worth in the Idaho eSports Arena!

Fundraising is in the ballpark! The plans are to raise enough money in order to fund our SSBU ventures, including discounted /full ride trips to the Boise Championships in the late spring, jerseys, a high-end capture card, and discounted/full ride trips to regional tournaments!

On the topic of jerseys, we are considering a scholastic application to eSports gear in order to obtain discounted jerseys and other GEMSTONE merchandise! More information will become available as we discuss futher our options and clear up our budget.
