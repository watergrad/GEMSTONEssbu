Fun Facts

Bio: Vasser has been playing Smash Bros. since 2017, way back when Sm4sh was still a thing. He sucked a lot, though. He started playing SSBU in 2019, a while after the games realease. Ever since he took off the training wheels (a.k.a. Kirby up-b), he started maining Ness out of love for the game Earthbound (not to mention his love for easy yet stylish combos). He still thinks he is the best player in the group. Don't worry, just give him a few more tourneys.
